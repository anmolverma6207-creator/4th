Dronacharya The Gym isn't a franchise gimmick — it's a chain of health clubs raised floor by floor on 20+ years of hands-on coaching in GTB Nagar, Delhi. Inside: full free-weight floors, a dedicated powerlifting rig, cable and plate-loaded machines, and a trophy wall that isn't for show — every medal in that case was earned by someone who trained right here.

We also run our own homemade supplement line — peanut butter, protein blends and clean nutrition made the same way we coach: no shortcuts, just quality you can trust.

The Founder
Bhupender Dhawan
Founder & Head Coach GTB Nagar, Delhi Mr. India Panel Collaborator

Two decades in this city's gym floors have made Bhupender Dhawan a name GTB Nagar trusts — not for slogans, but for the results standing behind the front desk trophy cabinet. He built Dronacharya The Gym around one idea: a coach's job is to make sure the plates keep going up, week after week, for everyone in the room.

That philosophy now runs training collaborations with national-level athletes — including sessions alongside Mr. India title-holder Mukesh Singh Ghalot — and a homemade supplement line developed specifically "for upcoming youngsters" chasing their first transformation.
